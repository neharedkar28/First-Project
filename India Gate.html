Capital Landmark

India Gate

India Gate is one of Delhi's best-known public landmarks and a popular stop for visitors exploring the capital.

Quick Facts

  • Located in New Delhi
  • Built as a war memorial
  • Popular in the evening for its open public space

About the Destination

India Gate is a large ceremonial arch in New Delhi built to honor soldiers of the British Indian Army. It stands at the center of a major boulevard and remains one of the city's most recognizable monuments.

The area around India Gate is open, lively, and especially attractive during the evening when families and travelers gather nearby. Its location also makes it easy to include in a Delhi sightseeing plan along with museums and government buildings.

For visitors, India Gate offers both historical value and a strong city atmosphere, making it one of the simplest landmarks to add to a short Delhi trip.
