Jammu and Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir is admired for lakes, mountain scenery, gardens, and some of the most memorable landscapes in India.

Quick Facts

  • Located in northern India
  • Known for lakes, valleys, and snow-covered mountains
  • Popular for scenic stays and nature tourism

About the Destination

Jammu and Kashmir is known for natural beauty on a grand scale, including lakes, valleys, gardens, and mountain views. It is often described as one of the most beautiful regions in the country.

Visitors are especially drawn to Srinagar, Dal Lake, Gulmarg, and Pahalgam, where houseboats, meadows, and alpine views create a very distinctive travel experience. Different seasons bring very different scenery, from snow to spring gardens.

For travelers who want quiet surroundings, cool air, and unforgettable views, Jammu and Kashmir remains one of the strongest destination choices in India.
