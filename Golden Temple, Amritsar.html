Spiritual Landmark

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple in Amritsar is admired for its spiritual importance, reflective waters, and striking golden exterior.

Quick Facts

  • Located in Amritsar, Punjab
  • One of the most important Sikh shrines
  • Known for peace, service, and beautiful architecture

About the Destination

The Golden Temple, also called Harmandir Sahib, is one of the most important religious places for Sikhs. Its golden sanctum, surrounded by a sacred pool, creates one of the most memorable views in Indian travel.

Visitors are often moved by the calm environment, devotional music, and community service traditions such as the langar, where free meals are served to people from all backgrounds.

Beyond its beauty, the Golden Temple represents equality, service, and faith, which is why it remains one of the most respected and visited places in the country.
